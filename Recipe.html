Mole Rojo

Ingredients Cooking Instructions
  • 3 tbs cooking oil
  • 4 dried chili guajillo (cleaned and stem removed)
  • 2 dried chili ancho (cleaned and stem removed)
  • 4 dried chili puya (cleaned and stem removed)
  • abuelita chocolate
  • 5 galleta maria (animal crackers)
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 tsp comino entero (whole cumin)
  • 1 tsp clavo entero (whole clove)
  • 1 tsp pimienta entero (whole pepper)
  • 1/4 white onion
  • 3-4 garlic ears
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • boiled chicken (roughly shredded or whole leg pieces)
  1. In a small frying pan heat oil on medium heat
  2. Set to low heat
  3. Very carefully add chiles (one type at a time)
  4. Gently fry for few seconds chilles will puff up and slightly change color
    (do not let chiles burn, turn a dark color, or sauce will be bitter)
  5. Remove chiles and set aside in blender
  6. In the same heated oil
  7. Fry oinions and garlic until fragrant, remove from heat and add to blender
  8. Slightly fry cookies and peanuts for about 30-60 seconds , remove from heat and add to blender
  9. Lasty toast comino, clavo, and pimienta
  10. Add spices to blender and add chicken broth
  11. break chocolate into small pieces and add to blender (amount is up to cook)
  12. Blend very well untill you get a sauce add more chicken broth if needed
  13. Add mole sauce over chicken and serve with rice

Tacos Dorados

Ingredients Cooking Instructions
  • Cooking oil (enough to fry)
  • Boiled chicken shredded
  • Corn tortillas (thin perferably)
  • Garnish:
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Thin sliced tomato
  • Sour cream
  • Cojita Cheese
  • Green Salsa
  • Sliced red raddish
  1. Slightly warm tortillas on flat griddle (comal)
  2. While tortilla is warm put chicken and roll (not too tight!)
  3. With a wooden toothpick combine 3-4 tacos together
  4. Create about 12 tacos (4 bundles of 3)
  5. In a small/medium frying pan heat up oil on medium high heat
  6. Fry tacos one bundle at a time untill tortilla turns a golden brown color
  7. Remove from oil and place on paper towel to drain
  8. Plate tacos and add garnish starting with sour cream and cheese

* This recipe can become vegetarian by replacing chicken with smashed boiled papa (potatoes)
boil and smash potatoes, potatoes should be chunky not smooth
add salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and black peper to taste
